Frequently asked questions

How do I connect?

Open LunaVPN and tap the power button. The first time, iOS will ask you to allow the VPN configuration — tap Allow. You'll be connected in a couple of seconds.

It won't connect — what should I do?

Make sure you have a working internet connection, then tap the power button again. If it still fails, fully close and reopen the app, or try again in a few minutes. Some heavily restricted networks may interfere with VPN connections.

How much free time do I get?

New users get 15 minutes of free connection time. Once it's used up, subscribe to LunaVPN Premium for unlimited access.

What does Premium cost?

LunaVPN Premium is available as a weekly or monthly subscription. You can see the current pricing on the Premium tab inside the app before you subscribe.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the app, go to the Premium tab, and tap Restore. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account you used to subscribe.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → LunaVPN → Cancel.

Do you keep logs of my activity?

No. LunaVPN runs a strict no-logs policy — we do not record the websites you visit, your DNS queries, or the content of your traffic. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
