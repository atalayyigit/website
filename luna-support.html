Frequently asked questions

How do I save a video?

Copy the link to the post, open Video Downloader, paste the link, and tap Download. Your video is added to the app's library and your Photos.

Where are my saved videos?

They appear in the Downloads tab inside the app, and are also saved to your device's Photos library when you grant photo access.

A download failed — what should I do?

Make sure the post is public and the link is complete, then try again. Links can expire, so a fresh copy of the link usually fixes it.

Why is a video not in the highest quality?

Video Downloader always downloads the best quality the source platform makes available. Some platforms only provide certain resolutions for a given video.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open the app, go to the Pro tab, and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple Account you used to subscribe.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → Video Downloader → Cancel.
